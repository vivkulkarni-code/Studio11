IMPORTANT INSTRUCTION
Do NOT build a digital PDF viewer.
Do NOT build a traditional salon rate card.
Build a premium luxury interactive experience where customers discover services, watch cinematic videos, create a personalized salon session, receive intelligent recommendations, and book seamlessly.
The final experience should feel like an Apple product showcase combined with a luxury fashion magazine and a modern booking platform while maintaining Studio11's black-and-gold premium identity.
